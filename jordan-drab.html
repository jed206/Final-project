About Me:

My name is Jordan Drab and I am currently a Junior at Rutgers University. I am majoring in Information Technology and minoring in DCIM. I was born May 1st, 1998 and am the oldest child of my family. I grew up in Suffern New York, which is right on the border of New Jersey. I have two younger siblings named Reilly and Callie. Reilly is currently a Freshman at the University of Vermont and Callie is a Freshman in High School. My parents’ names are Kirsten and Michael. My mom grew up on Long Island while my dad grew up in Suffern. They both met in college at C.W. Post on Long Island. My mom does not work anymore but my dad is an Elementary School Gym Teacher and has been for over 20 years now. My sisters and I all had him for gym class, which was something we enjoyed when we were younger. I have two small dogs that are a mix of miniature pinscher that are named Max and Perri. They are brother and sister and can not live without each other.

He became one of the Varsity coaches at Suffern my Freshman year. My sister Reilly was moved up to Varsity in eighth grade. The team was nationally ranked and I played in three State Final Four games. My grade played so well together, which helped us all get recruited by colleges. I committed to play Lacrosse at Rutgers my Sophomore year. It never felt real until it was finally move in day three years later. Reilly also got recruited to play Lacrosse and is a member of the team at Vermont. Lacrosse is usually the main topic of conversation at the dinner table, which gets tiring for my poor mom. Now, Callie is in the process of getting recruited and my whole family is along for the ride.
