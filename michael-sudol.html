About me:

My name is Michael Sudol and I am currently a senior at Rutgers University - New Brunswick studying Information Technology and Informatics and Digital, Communication, Information, and Media (DCIM). Most people that know me call me by my last name Sudol, which started back in elementary school and followed me into college which I didn't expect.
